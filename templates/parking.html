Thank you all for registering for eHacks 2023. We are excited to meet everyone! A couple of quick notes regarding the event:

Discord

If you have not joined our Discord server, please do so here: {{discord_link}}. We will be using Discord as our main form of communication and will be releasing any important announcements regarding the event through Discord. It's also a space that you can use to communicate with other hackers.

Parking

Parking is very scarce around the venue. The parking lot around the venue is unfortunately not available to students. Street parking is an option, but it is not free the entire weekend and also not recommended. There are some parking lots around the venue (Link to Google Maps Parking Info), but their price fluctuates based on the events going on in the city.

If you are an SIUE Student...

we really recommend taking the bus. If you had marked that you were planning on providing your own transportation, you can have that changed by replying to this email indicating that you would like to take the bus by February 24th.

Parking in the SIUE red lots is free over the weekend. We will send out more information regarding specific bus details in the coming days.

If you aren't an SIUE Student...

we really recommend carpooling if possible. Unfortunately, we do not have much control over the parking situation. If you are attending from St. Louis, the T-Rex Innovation Center is a few blocks away from a couple of MetroLink Stations (8th & Pine and Convention Center).

The MetroBus also has a couple of routes that go near T-Rex. Here is a link to a map of the city's Metro routes: Link to St. Louis Metro System Map. T-Rex is located on Washington Ave, in between N 9th St and N 10th St and next to "The Dome at America's Center" (Link to T-Rex on Google Maps).

If you have any questions before the event, feel free to email siue.ehacks@gmail.com, or reach out to anyone with the "Organizer" role in the Discord server.
