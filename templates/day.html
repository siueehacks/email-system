Things to Bring

The venue will not have any beds or showers. If you plan on staying overnight, here are some things to bring:

  • Laptop (or something to program with)
  • Laptop and Phone chargers
  • Sleeping stuff (blankets, pillows, sleeping bag, etc.)
  • Toothbrush/toothpaste
  • Deodorant
  • Change of clothes for two days
  • ID
  • Hardware (if you are interested in doing hardware based project)

Things NOT to bring:

  • Virus of any kind. If you are sick, stay home
  • Anything illegal. (Please use common sense)

Bus

For those taking the bus, it will leave from the circle right outside of the engineering building on SIUE's campus at 4:20pm on Friday. It will make a trip Friday and then make a trip Sunday, leaving from T-Rex at 1:00pm. Only those who have inicated that they would like to take the bus will be able to take the bus to T-Rex.

Parking

There will not be any parking at the event venue. There will be paid street parking throughout the city (although street parking is not recommended). There are also various parking locations around the venue (Link to Google Maps Parking Info), but their price fluctuates based on the events going on in the city.
